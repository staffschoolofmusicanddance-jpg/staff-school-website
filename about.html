Our Story

Founded in 2022 by a musician, educator, and Trinity-certified instructor.

STAFF School of Music & Dance was founded in 2022 by Mr. Ram Reddy, a passionate musician, educator, and certified Keyboard and Guitar instructor from Trinity College London.

His vision was to create a professional and welcoming academy where students could develop musical and dance skills, confidence, creativity, discipline, and stage presence through structured training.

Today, the academy offers Beginner to Senior Level pathways for children, teenagers, and adults in both music and dance disciplines.
