Privacy Policy

Editable privacy guidance for enquiries and demo requests.

This privacy page is intentionally simple and should be reviewed by the academy owner before the site is published with live forms or real contact data.

Owner review required

Confirm how long enquiry records are kept, who can access them, and whether third-party tools are used.

What information may be collected

The academy may collect names, phone numbers, WhatsApp numbers, email addresses, student age, course interest, preferred timing, previous experience, and message details through demo or enquiry forms.

Why the information is collected

Information may be used to respond to enquiries, arrange demo classes, suggest course levels, confirm batches, and communicate about admissions or scheduling updates.

How the academy may contact you

The academy may contact users by phone, WhatsApp, or email using the details they voluntarily submit.

Google Forms and third-party tools

If the owner adds Google Forms or another supported form service, submitted data may also be processed by that service under its own privacy policy.

Correction or deletion requests

Users should be given a way to request correction or deletion of information by contacting the academy directly. Add the final response process here once confirmed by the owner.

Owner action before publishing

Replace placeholders, confirm local privacy expectations, and make sure the policy reflects the actual tools and processes used by the academy.
