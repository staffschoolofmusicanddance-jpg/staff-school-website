About STAFF

A structured academy founded to help students grow in confidence and creativity.

STAFF School of Music & Dance was founded in 2022 by Mr. Ram Reddy, a certified Keyboard and Guitar instructor through Trinity College London. His vision was to create a professional and welcoming academy where students could build skills, discipline, expression, and stage presence.

The academy serves children, teenagers, and adults through clear progression from Beginner to Senior Level, combining structured teaching with joyful learning.
